✦Before You Begin
Overview

Esther reads like a royal drama — full of banquets, plots, a beauty contest, a gallows, and a sudden reversal of fortune. But beneath the palace intrigue runs one quiet truth: God is working even when His name is never spoken.

The name of God does not appear once in the entire book of Esther. Yet His fingerprints are everywhere — in the timing, the "coincidences," the sleepless night of a king. This is the book's great teaching device: providence works in the shadows.
